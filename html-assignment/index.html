About Me

Hi there! This is Kei from Hong Kong. You can call me Karina. Interactive Media Design is not my 1st post-secondary educational program. I studied LA as my degree program back in HK. During my studies, visual communication skills weakened my performance. It resulted in my visual presentation being inferior to the verbal one on each studio project. It made me feel stressed, so I did not graduate.

Interactive Media Design provided by Algonquin College covers a wide range of topics. I believe that I will be able to gain a deeper understanding and comprehensive training in communicating with people through different mediums and visual presentations. It is why I am here. I enjoy all the classes so far, and I am glad I made the decision for enrolling this course.

I would call myself old-fashion, because I prefer to create art pieces by hand-drawing rather than using software. This course is definitely a big challenge to me. But I am confident I can do it well.

True confidence is living in uncertainty and moving forward. -Phil Stutz
